Secure Cloud Hosting

Professional hosting for your accounting software on hardened, well-equipped servers — with automatic backups and a dedicated control panel for every subscriber.

About the Service

Hosting solutions at enterprise grade

FINEXSA Capital delivers a professional cloud hosting service designed for companies running advanced accounting software. Whether the platform is Al-Mizan, Onyx, SAP, Oracle, or any other accounting system — we guarantee a secure, fast, and reliable environment around the clock.

What sets us apart is that every subscriber receives a dedicated control panel, granting direct access to their backup folder, the ability to download backups at any time, and live monitoring of their server status — all under an independent, password-protected account.
